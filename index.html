Who are we?

In the world of competitive sports, injuries, sprains, and pains are all too common, with ankle sprains being particularly prevalent.

HUGX is a performance wear company dedicated to support, compression, comfort, and peak performance. We are excited to introduce our very first product, specifically designed for basketball players.

  • We are excited to introduce our very first product, specifically designed for basketball players.
  • While we often focus on the importance of shoes, we tend to overlook another crucial component of our gear: our socks.
  • The added garments also meant more laundry. This led us to create a solution specifically for basketball players tha addresses these issues on a physical, mental, and emotional level.

Our socks are also dry-fit and antibacterial, ensuring freshness and odor control.
